      My 100th review goes to my favorite Starbuck's location ever. I don't consider myself a "coffee snob" but I am a regular at this particular coffee shop. The entire staff is always so friendly and they really know how to make my day. Love this staff and location!

      I really do enjoy Starbucks beverages, but the State Street location is never a fun experience. On a recent visit, they had a promotion where they were giving stickers when you bought a holiday beverage. The cashier told me he couldn't give me a sticker because "they were out." The promotion earned you a free holiday beverage for every 5 you bought. Rather than contacting a manager or giving me a discount, he simply told me it wasn't his problem. On a different visit, the baristas were talking about some of the items in the display case and saying how terribly they taste. If you want Starbucks and you're downtown, go to the one on MLK near the Capitol.
